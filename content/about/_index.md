## Who We Are

Wikimedians of Kerala User Group is a recognized Wikimedia user group consisting of volunteer editors, photographers, and free knowledge advocates from Kerala, India. We are part of the global Wikimedia movement that operates Wikipedia, Wikimedia Commons, Wikidata, and other free knowledge projects.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to:

- Increase the availability and quality of free knowledge in Malayalam
- Document Kerala's rich cultural heritage, history, and biodiversity
- Train and mentor new contributors to Wikimedia projects
- Organize community events, edit-a-thons, and photo walks
- Collaborate with educational institutions and cultural organizations

## Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone can freely access the sum of all human knowledge in their own language. We work towards making Malayalam Wikipedia and other Wikimedia projects comprehensive, accurate, and accessible to all.

## Recognition

Wikimedians of Kerala was officially recognized as a Wikimedia User Group in 2015. Since then, we have grown to become one of the most active Wikimedia communities in India.

## Join Us

We welcome anyone interested in contributing to free knowledge. Whether you're an experienced editor or completely new to Wikipedia, there's a place for you in our community.

Connect with us through our social media channels or attend one of our events to get started!
