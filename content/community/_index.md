## Our Community

Wikimedians of Kerala is a diverse community of volunteers from across Kerala. Our members include:

- Students and educators
- Photographers and artists
- Researchers and academics
- Technology professionals
- Cultural activists
- Language enthusiasts

## Active Members

Our community has over 500 active members who regularly contribute to Wikimedia projects. Some of our most active contributors have made tens of thousands of edits to Malayalam Wikipedia and uploaded thousands of photographs to Wikimedia Commons.

## How to Join

Joining our community is easy:

1. **Create a Wikipedia Account**: Sign up for a free account on Wikipedia
2. **Join Our Online Groups**: Connect with us on social media platforms
3. **Attend Events**: Participate in our edit-a-thons and meetups
4. **Start Contributing**: Begin editing articles or uploading photos

## Community Guidelines

We follow Wikimedia's friendly space policy and expect all members to:

- Respect other contributors
- Assume good faith
- Be welcoming to newcomers
- Follow Wikipedia's policies and guidelines
- Collaborate constructively

## Regional Chapters

We have active contributors across Kerala, with regular meetups in:

- Thiruvananthapuram
- Kochi
- Kozhikode
- Thrissur
- Kannur
- Malappuram

## Get Involved

There are many ways to contribute:

- **Edit Articles**: Improve existing articles or create new ones
- **Upload Photos**: Share your photographs on Wikimedia Commons
- **Translate**: Translate articles from other languages
- **Organize Events**: Help organize local meetups and edit-a-thons
- **Mentor Others**: Guide new contributors
- **Spread Awareness**: Promote free knowledge in your community
