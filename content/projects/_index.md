## Malayalam Wikipedia

Our primary focus is expanding and improving Malayalam Wikipedia. We organize regular edit-a-thons and training sessions to:

- Create new articles on notable topics
- Improve existing articles with citations and references
- Translate quality articles from other language Wikipedias
- Add more content about Kerala's culture, history, and people

Malayalam Wikipedia currently has over 90,000 articles and continues to grow with our community's contributions.

## Wikimedia Commons

We conduct photography projects and photo walks to document:

- Historical monuments and heritage sites
- Cultural events and festivals
- Biodiversity and natural landscapes
- Notable personalities and events

All photographs are uploaded to Wikimedia Commons under free licenses, making them available for use in Wikipedia articles and other educational purposes.

## Wikidata Projects

We contribute structured data about Kerala to Wikidata:

- Adding information about places, people, and organizations
- Connecting Malayalam Wikipedia articles to Wikidata items
- Improving data quality and adding references
- Creating queries and visualizations using Wikidata

## Education Programs

We partner with educational institutions to:

- Conduct Wikipedia workshops for students and teachers
- Integrate Wikipedia editing into academic curricula
- Organize campus edit-a-thons and competitions
- Provide training and mentorship for student editors

## Cultural Heritage Documentation

We work on documenting Kerala's intangible cultural heritage:

- Traditional art forms and performing arts
- Folk songs and oral traditions
- Traditional knowledge and practices
- Historical narratives and local histories
