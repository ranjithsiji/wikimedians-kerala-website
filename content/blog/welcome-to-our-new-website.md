We're excited to announce the launch of our new website for Wikimedians of Kerala User Group!

This platform will serve as a central hub for our community, showcasing our projects, activities, and the incredible work done by Wikimedians across Kerala.

## What's New

- **Modern Design**: A fresh, responsive design that works beautifully on all devices
- **Project Showcase**: Detailed information about our ongoing and past projects
- **Event Calendar**: Stay updated with our latest activities and events
- **Community Blog**: Regular updates, stories, and insights from our members
- **Easy Contact**: Multiple ways to connect with us and get involved

## Join Our Community

Whether you're a seasoned Wikipedian or just curious about contributing to free knowledge, we welcome you to join our community. Check out our [Community](/community/) page to learn how to get started.

## Stay Connected

Follow us on social media and subscribe to our mailing list to stay updated with our activities and announcements.

Together, let's continue building free knowledge for everyone!
