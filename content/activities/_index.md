## Regular Activities

### Edit-a-thons

We organize regular edit-a-thons where contributors gather to:

- Create and improve Wikipedia articles on specific topics
- Learn editing skills from experienced editors
- Collaborate on large-scale content improvement projects
- Compete in friendly editing challenges

### Photo Walks

Our photography events focus on:

- Documenting heritage sites and monuments
- Capturing cultural events and festivals
- Wildlife and nature photography
- Portrait photography of notable personalities

### Training Workshops

We conduct regular training sessions on:

- Wikipedia editing basics for beginners
- Advanced editing techniques
- Image editing and uploading to Commons
- Wikidata contribution
- Copyright and free licensing

### Monthly Meetups

Community members meet regularly to:

- Discuss ongoing projects
- Plan future events
- Share experiences and learn from each other
- Build community bonds

## Major Events

### Wikicamp Kerala

An annual gathering of Wikimedians from across Kerala featuring:

- Keynote presentations
- Skill-building workshops
- Project planning sessions
- Networking opportunities

### Wiki Loves Monuments

An annual photography competition to document:

- Historical monuments
- Archaeological sites
- Protected heritage buildings

This is the world's largest photography competition and we actively participate every year.

### Malayalam Wikipedia Day

Celebrating Malayalam Wikipedia's anniversary with:

- Special edit-a-thons
- Recognition of top contributors
- Community celebrations

### Campus Programs

We regularly visit colleges and universities for:

- Wikipedia awareness sessions
- Student edit-a-thons
- Faculty training programs
- Academic collaboration projects
